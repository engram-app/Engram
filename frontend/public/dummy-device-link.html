Engram Device-Link Dummy Client

Pretends to be the Obsidian plugin starting a device-flow link. Runs POST /api/auth/device to mint a user_code, then sends you to /link?code=… with your existing Engram session. Use this to exercise concurrent_devices_cap on Free tier — first vault link works, second 402s at Authorize.

Pre-flight:

You must be signed in to the Engram app at localhost:5173 in this same browser. Free tier syncs files between 1 device at a time, so the second link will hit the cap dialog.

Ready. Click "Start device-flow + open /link" to mint a code and go to the link page.
